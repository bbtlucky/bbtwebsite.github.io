Naruto Watching Order (Important)

  1. Naruto (2002) – The beginning of Naruto Uzumaki’s ninja journey.
  2. Naruto Shippuden (2007) – Emotional arcs, powerful battles, and the Great Ninja War.
  3. Naruto Movies – Optional but enjoyable.
For the best experience, complete Naruto first and then start Shippuden.

Pro Tips for New Viewers

  • Be patient — Naruto is a long anime.
  • Watch with focus and emotion to connect with characters.
  • You can skip filler episodes for faster watching.
  • English dub is easier for beginners.
  • Sub version gives original Japanese voice emotion.

Naruto is not just an anime. It is an emotional journey about friendship, hard work, pain, dreams, and never giving up.

If you watch seriously, you will fall in love with the characters.
